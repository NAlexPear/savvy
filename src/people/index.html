The Savvy Coders Team

Meet our Instructors and Admin Team



Instructors

Alex Pearson

St. Louis

Alex is one of the co-founders of Savvy Coders and the primary author of the course curriculum. It's Alex's mission to help others grow as developers through formal instruction, personal mentorship, and contributions to the software development community. He currently works as a front-end developer for Five-Star Technologies, focusing on application development with Backbone.js. You can read more on his blog, or take a look at some of his open source contributions on GitHub.

James Logan

Nashville

James is the lead instructor for Savvy Coders' Nashville cohort. A graduate of the Nashville Software School, James immediately jumped into teaching, and is proud to be giving back to the Nashville technology scene through Savvy Coders. During the day, James works at Metova, an application development shop that specializes in creating software solutions for clients of every size. James focuses on building apps with Node, Ionic, and more. Follow James on GitHub here.
